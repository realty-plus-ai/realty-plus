About Realty+

Helping Real Estate Businesses Communicate Smarter

Realty+ is an AI-powered communication platform that enables real estate businesses to automate customer conversations using the official WhatsApp Business Platform while keeping brokers in control of every relationship.

Built For Modern Real Estate Businesses

Real estate professionals spend a significant amount of time responding to repetitive customer questions, collecting buyer requirements, following up with leads, and manually updating CRM systems.

Realty+ was created to automate these repetitive workflows so brokers can spend more time closing deals and helping customers make informed buying decisions.

Rather than replacing sales professionals, Realty+ acts as an intelligent assistant that handles repetitive communication while allowing brokers to step in whenever human expertise matters most.

Our Principles

  • Automate repetitive work without replacing human relationships.
  • Provide fast and professional customer communication.
  • Use AI responsibly to improve operational efficiency.
  • Keep businesses in control of customer conversations.
  • Design software that integrates with existing business workflows.
What Realty+ Does

One Platform For Every Customer Conversation

Lead Qualification

Automatically collect customer requirements including property type, preferred location, budget, timeline, and buying intent.

Automated Follow-ups

Continue engaging potential buyers until meaningful human interaction is required.

Property Matching

Recommend properties that align with customer requirements while helping brokers stay organized.

Broker Notifications

Instantly notify sales teams when customers demonstrate strong buying intent.
